BELTON, MO DMV

The Belton, MO DMV (officially the Belton License Office) has moved to a new location as of August 2025. 

**Missouri Department of Revenue (.gov) +1**

**Address:**347 N Scott AveBelton, MO 64012 

**Hours of Operation:**

- **Monday - Friday:** 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

- **Saturday & Sunday:** Closed 

**Key Details:**

- **Contact Number:** (816) 331-9400

- **Services:** Motor vehicle registration, license plates, and driver's licensing.

- **Wait Times:** Users report wait times can range from 10 to over 30 minutes, with busy times often in the afternoon.

- **Accepted Payments:** Checks, credit/debit cards, and NFC mobile payments. 

*Note: The old location at 325 Main St is closed.* 

To renew your car registration in Belton, MO (Cass County), you will need your renewal notice, proof of insurance, a paid personal property tax receipt for the previous year (or a statement of non-assessment), and a valid safety/emissions inspection (if applicable, typically within 60 days).

**Missouri Department of Revenue (.gov) +4**

**Key Information:**

- **Where to Renew:** You can renew in person at a Missouri license office (e.g., in Belton or nearby Grandview), by mail, or online.

- **Property Tax Info:** If you paid taxes after your county's deadline, you may need to present the paper receipt to the DOR.

- **Non-Assessment:** If you did not owe taxes (e.g., new resident, first-time car owner), obtain a statement of non-assessment from the [Cass County Assessor's office](https://www.casscounty.com/2274/Certificate-of-Non-Assessment).
