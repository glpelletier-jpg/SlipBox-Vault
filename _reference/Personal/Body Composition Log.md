# Body Composition Log

Weekly body composition measurements taken via Galaxy Watch Ultra BIA scan (Home + Back buttons, ~15 seconds).

**Best practice:** Measure Sunday morning, before eating, after using the bathroom. Same conditions every week for consistent trend data.

**Note:** BIA readings shift with hydration — don't measure after exercise, alcohol, or when dehydrated. A 1–2% variation week-to-week is normal noise; look at 4-week trends, not weekly changes.

---

## How to measure

1. Wear watch 2–3 cm higher on your wrist than normal
2. Press **Home button + Back button simultaneously**
3. Hold completely still for ~15 seconds
4. Results appear on watch and sync to Samsung Health → Body Composition
