## Major discovery: literature notes have two formats

The 38 literature notes split into two distinct types:

**Type B — 31 detailed chapter summaries:** These were imported from a previous PKM system and contain rich, chapter-by-chapter content. These are actually the most valuable material for generating permanent notes — mining them is the priority, not reformatting them.
