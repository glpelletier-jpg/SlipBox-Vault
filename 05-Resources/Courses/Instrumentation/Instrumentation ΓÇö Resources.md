# Instrumentation — Resources

## Start here

1. **NAMUR NE 43** — free from namur.net; defines fault signal levels for 4–20 mA
2. **NAMUR NE 107** — device self-monitoring symbols; the universal diagnostic language
3. **Endress+Hauser Heartbeat Technology White Paper** — free from E+H; explains continuous verification
