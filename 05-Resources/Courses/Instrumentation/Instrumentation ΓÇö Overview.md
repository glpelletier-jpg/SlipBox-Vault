# Instrumentation — Overview

> Self-study course covering field instrumentation — signal standards (4–20 mA, HART, IO-Link), device selection, calibration, SIL certification, and vendor-specific knowledge for Endress+Hauser, Pepperl+Fuchs, and SICK. Directly applicable to Argenta-Water and CCPC-1250.

---

## Goal

**Credential / outcome:** CAP measurement/instrumentation domain readiness; applied field competency across Argenta vendors
**Why this matters:** E+H, P+F, and SICK devices are on every Argenta line. Understanding signal chains, diagnostics, and SIL certification is essential for commissioning, troubleshooting, and validation.
