# Portable Operating for Amateur Radio



A good hardcopy resource for the rural operator is the DeLorme Atlas & Gazetteer Paper Maps series of books (buy.garmin.com/en-US/US/p/575993). Each book covers a single state or two adjacent states and includes both road and topographic maps with latitude and longitude. If you find yourself traveling rural roads, hiking trails, or locating lakes to set up a portable shack, the DeLorme Atlas is an excellent guide to have with you.
