# Process Safety (IEC 61511) — Resources

---

## Start here (highest ROI)

1. **IEC 61511-1 (2016)** — the standard itself; read Part 1 clause by clause alongside this course
2. **ISA-84.00.01** — US equivalent; identical content, easier to source domestically
3. **LOPA: Layer of Protection Analysis (Simplified Process Risk Assessment)** — AIChE CCPS book; best practical LOPA reference
