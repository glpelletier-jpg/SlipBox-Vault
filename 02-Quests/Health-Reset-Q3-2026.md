# Health Reset Q3 2026

## Why this quest

> My health is the foundation everything else runs on — my energy as a parent, my creative output, my focus at work. Right now it's the most neglected area in my life. This quest is about building the baseline I need to show up well everywhere else.

## Definition of done

> I will know this quest is complete when: I have exercised at least 4x/week for 8 consecutive weeks, I'm asleep by a consistent bedtime 6 out of 7 nights, and I've cut out the dietary habits that are draining my energy — and I feel the difference.
