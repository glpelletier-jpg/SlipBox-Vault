> Spine and medical issues are real constraints on energy. Factor this into how much I take on — this is a depth quest, not a volume quest.

## What I'll say no to

> Taking on tasks I don't understand well enough to do properly just to look busy. Saying "I'll figure it out" without actually asking for help when stuck. Skipping the documentation habit when I'm short on time — that's exactly when it matters most.
