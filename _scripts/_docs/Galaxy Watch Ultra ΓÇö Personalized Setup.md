## 1. Switch Home Button to Google Gemini

**Why:** Bixby is limited. Gemini handles natural language better — dictate a work observation mid-task without typing. Also gives you a capable AI assistant independently via LTE when your phone is away.

**Time required:** 2 minutes

### Steps

**On the watch:**

1. Press the **Home button** to wake the watch
2. Swipe down from the watch face to open the **Quick Settings panel**
3. Scroll down and tap the **Settings gear** icon
4. Scroll down to **Buttons and gestures** and tap it
5. Tap **Home button**
6. Tap **Press and hold**
7. The current setting shows "Bixby" — tap it to see the list of options
8. Select **Google Gemini**
9. Confirm if prompted

**Test it:** Press and hold the Home button for 1–2 seconds. Gemini opens instead of Bixby. Speak a test command: *"Remind me to log BP when I get home"* — it will create a reminder.

**To use for work notes:** Press and hold Home → say *"Note: CP 12 agitator — checked coupling, no visible wear"* → Gemini will save or speak it back. For a permanent capture, follow up with dictating it into Todoist (next section).

## 2. Set Quick Button to Outdoor Walk (One-Tap Habit Start)

**Why:** Your Health Reset Month 1 milestone is 30 min of movement every day. The biggest friction killer is the steps between "I should go walk" and "I'm actually walking." This makes it one press.

**Time required:** 3 minutes

### Steps

**On your S25 Ultra:**

1. Open the **Galaxy Wearable** app
2. Tap your **Galaxy Watch Ultra** at the top if it isn't already selected
3. Tap **Watch settings**
4. Tap **Buttons and gestures**
5. Scroll down to the **Quick button** section
6. Tap **Single press** (the first option)
7. You'll see a list of apps and actions — scroll down to find **Samsung Health**
8. Tap **Samsung Health** to expand its options
9. Select **Workout** → then select **Outdoor walk**
10. Tap **Save** or **Done**

### Test it

Go outside (or just step outside briefly). Press the Quick Button once. Samsung Health should open directly on the Outdoor Walk start screen — just tap the play button and you're tracking.

### Optional: Double press → Stopwatch

While you're in the same menu, set **Double press** to **Stopwatch**. Useful for timing tasks, equipment cycles, or on-site observations at Argenta without opening your phone.

1. Tap **Double press** in the same Buttons and gestures screen
2. Select **Stopwatch**
3. Save

## 3. Set Up Samsung Health Activity Goal (30-Min Movement Habit)

**Why:** Your quest habit is "Moved for 30+ min." Setting a goal means the watch actively tracks progress toward it throughout the day and alerts you when you haven't moved. You'll see it on the watch face and in Samsung Health.

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Part A — Set the activity goal on your S25 Ultra

1. Open **Samsung Health** on your S25 Ultra
2. Tap the **Activity** card on the home screen (shows your step ring or daily summary)
3. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** in the top right corner
4. Tap **Settings** or **Goal settings**
5. You'll see goal options — set:
   - **Daily steps:** 10,000 (or 7,500 if 10k feels aggressive at first — you can raise it)
   - **Active time:** 30 minutes
6. Tap **Save**

### Part B — Add active time to your watch face

1. On your watch, press and hold the watch face to enter edit mode
2. Tap your current watch face to edit its complications
3. Find an empty complication slot (usually bottom or left)
4. Tap it → scroll through options → select **Active time** or **Activity rings**
5. Press the Home button to save and exit

Now you'll see your daily active minutes on your wrist throughout the day as a passive reminder.

### Part C — Enable inactivity alerts

When you've been sitting still for too long (useful during desk work or office time at Argenta), the watch will tap you.

1. On your watch: **Settings** → scroll down to **Advanced features**
2. Tap **Inactive time alert**
3. Toggle it **On**
4. Set the interval to **60 minutes** (alerts after 60 min of no movement)

## 4. Set Up Bedtime Reminder (Sleep Consistency Milestone)

**Why:** Your quest requires hitting a target bedtime 5/7 nights. The bottleneck isn't intention — it's the watch tapping you when you've lost track of time. Set this up once, let it run.

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Step 1 — Set your sleep goal and bedtime

1. Open **Samsung Health** on your S25 Ultra
2. Tap the **Sleep** card on the home screen
3. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** → tap **Settings**
4. Tap **Sleep goal**
5. Set:
   - **Target sleep time:** 7–8 hours (whatever your target is)
   - **Wake-up time:** your consistent wake-up time (e.g., 6:00 AM)
   - Samsung Health will calculate your target bedtime automatically (e.g., 10:30 PM for 7.5h + time to fall asleep)
6. Tap **Save**

### Step 2 — Enable Bedtime guidance

1. Still in Samsung Health → Sleep settings
2. Tap **Bedtime guidance** and toggle it **On**
3. Set the reminder to fire **30 minutes before** your calculated bedtime
4. The watch will vibrate and show a gentle "Time to wind down" notification at that time

### Step 3 — Enable Sleep mode scheduling

This automatically reduces watch battery drain and silences non-emergency notifications during your sleep window.

1. On your watch: **Settings** → **Battery** → **Sleep mode**
2. Tap **Schedule**
3. Toggle **Schedule** on
4. Set **Start time** to your bedtime (e.g., 11:00 PM)
5. Set **End time** to your wake time (e.g., 6:30 AM)
6. Save

### Step 4 — Ensure watch is charged for sleep tracking

Sleep tracking requires the watch on your wrist all night. You need ≥30% battery before bed.

**Habit:** Charge the watch for 20–30 minutes while eating dinner or doing your evening routine. Magnetic charger on the nightstand or kitchen counter — wherever you'll actually use it.

## 5. Calibrate Blood Pressure Monitor

**Why:** Your daily note template has `bp_systolic:` and `bp_diastolic:` fields — you intend to log BP. The watch can only measure blood pressure after calibration with a physical arm cuff. Without calibration, the BP feature is locked.

**What you need:** A standard upper-arm blood pressure cuff (omron, Withings, or similar — available for ~$30–50). You use it once to calibrate, then the watch tracks BP independently.

**Time required:** 15–20 minutes (including the 3 cuff readings)

### Step 1 — Install Samsung Health Monitor

1. On your S25 Ultra, open the **Play Store**
2. Search **Samsung Health Monitor**
3. Install it (free, Samsung app — separate from Samsung Health)
4. Open it and accept permissions

### Step 2 — Check eligibility (one-time)

1. In Samsung Health Monitor, tap **Blood Pressure**
2. Tap **Set up** if this is your first time
3. You'll be asked to enter: age, height, weight, and medical history questions
4. Complete these accurately — they affect measurement accuracy

### Step 3 — Prepare for cuff readings

Before taking cuff readings, you must:
- Sit quietly for **5 minutes** (no exercise, no caffeine in the last 30 min)
- Sit with back supported, feet flat on the floor
- Rest your left arm on a flat surface at heart level
- Put the cuff on your left upper arm, 2–3 cm above the elbow

### Step 4 — Take 3 cuff readings

1. Take your first cuff reading — note the systolic/diastolic numbers
2. Wait **1 minute**
3. Take a second reading
4. Wait **1 minute**
5. Take a third reading
6. **Average the three** (the app will do this for you — just enter each reading)

### Step 5 — Enter readings into Samsung Health Monitor

1. In Samsung Health Monitor → Blood Pressure → **Calibrate**
2. Enter each of your 3 readings when prompted
3. Tap **Save** — the app confirms calibration is complete

### Step 6 — Take your first watch-only reading

1. Sit still for 5 minutes
2. Open Samsung Health Monitor on your **watch** (not phone)
3. Tap **Blood Pressure** → tap **Measure**
4. Hold your arm still at heart level — the watch measures for ~30 seconds
5. Result appears on screen and syncs to your phone

### Recalibration reminder

Calibration expires every **28 days**. Samsung Health Monitor will remind you when it's time. Put the arm cuff somewhere you'll see it (nightstand) — you'll need it monthly.

### Logging to your vault

After measuring on the watch, open your daily note and enter values in:
```
bp_systolic: 118
bp_diastolic: 76
pulse: 72
```
Measure at the same time each day (morning, before eating) for consistent trend data.

## 6. Install Todoist on the Watch

**Why:** Todoist is your task system. Having it on the watch means you can check today's tasks without your phone and — critically — add a quick work observation or capture when you're on the Argenta plant floor with both hands on a job.

**Time required:** 5 minutes

### Step 1 — Install Todoist on the watch

**Option A — From the watch:**
1. Press the **Home button** to open the apps screen
2. Scroll to find the **Play Store** app and open it
3. Search for **Todoist**
4. Tap **Install**
5. Wait for download — takes 1–3 minutes depending on connection

**Option B — From your S25 Ultra (easier):**
1. Open **Galaxy Wearable** app on your phone
2. Tap **Apps** at the bottom
3. Tap **All apps** (or search for Todoist)
4. Find Todoist and tap **Install on watch**

### Step 2 — Sign in to Todoist on the watch

1. On the watch, open the newly installed **Todoist** app
2. It will prompt you to sign in — either scan a QR code from the Todoist phone app or enter your credentials
3. Select **Sign in with Google** (easiest) and use your Gmail account (glpelletier@gmail.com)

### Step 3 — Add Todoist as a quick tile

1. Swipe through your watch tiles to the far right
2. Scroll down to **Edit tiles** (or go to Galaxy Wearable → Watch settings → Tiles)
3. Tap **Add tile**
4. Find **Todoist** and add it
5. Drag it up near the top of your tile order — second or third position

### Step 4 — Add a task via voice (the key plant-floor use case)

The fastest way to capture a work observation when you don't have your phone out:

1. Press and hold **Home button** to open Gemini
2. Say: *"Add to Todoist: [your observation]"*
   - Example: *"Add to Todoist: Check oil levels on CP 12 gearbox next Tuesday"*
   - Example: *"Add to Todoist: Follow up with Mark about the SCADA access credentials"*
3. Gemini adds it to Todoist inbox — review and move to the right project later

Alternatively, open Todoist directly on the watch → tap the **+** button → use the microphone icon to dictate.

## 7. Install Spotify and Pre-Download a Playlist

**Why:** Your 30-min daily walk is the core Health Reset Month 1 habit. Walking with music — without needing your phone — reduces friction. You can leave the phone at home or at your desk and just go.

**Time required:** 10 minutes setup, then 5–10 minutes to download playlist (runs in background)

### Step 1 — Install Spotify on the watch

**From your S25 Ultra:**
1. Open **Galaxy Wearable** → **Apps**
2. Search **Spotify**
3. Tap **Install on watch**

Or install directly from the Play Store on the watch (same process as Todoist above).

### Step 2 — Sign in to Spotify on the watch

1. Open **Spotify** on the watch
2. Tap **Log in** → select **Log in with phone**
3. Your phone will prompt you to authorize — tap **Yes, log in**
4. Spotify is now connected

### Step 3 — Pre-download a playlist for offline use

You need to download your playlist to the watch's storage (32 GB available) so it plays without LTE.

1. On your **S25 Ultra**, open **Spotify**
2. Open a playlist you want on your watch
3. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** → tap **Allow downloading on watch**
4. Or: on the watch, open Spotify → find the playlist → tap the **download icon** (arrow pointing down)
5. Download runs in the background while watch is connected to Wi-Fi or your phone

**Recommended for walks:** 30–45 minute playlist (albums, podcast episodes, or a Spotify Daily Mix).

### Step 4 — Pair Bluetooth earbuds directly to the watch

For phone-free walks, your earbuds need to connect to the **watch**, not your phone.

1. Put your earbuds in pairing mode
2. On the watch: **Settings** → **Connections** → **Bluetooth**
3. Tap **Scan** → select your earbuds from the list
4. They're now paired to the watch

**Note:** When your earbuds are connected to the watch, audio from your phone will not play through them until you switch back. Reconnect to phone via the earbuds' Bluetooth settings or the Galaxy Wearable app.

### Step 5 — Walk routine with the watch

1. Quick Button (single press) → Outdoor Walk starts tracking
2. Swipe to Spotify tile → tap play on your downloaded playlist
3. Walk for 30 min
4. Quick Button again (or tap End on watch) → save workout
5. Quest habit ✅ — log it in your daily note tonight

## 8. Water Lock Workflow for the Plant Floor

**Why:** Pharma manufacturing environments involve washdown, cleaning chemicals, and wet surfaces. The watch is rated for this, but activating Water Lock prevents accidental screen presses and sensor errors during wet conditions.

**Time required:** 30 seconds to activate anytime

### When to activate Water Lock at Argenta

Activate before:
- CIP (Clean-in-Place) cycles near open equipment
- Hosing down or pressure washing an area
- Working in a wet-process room (mixing, filling, washing)
- Hand-washing at the plant sink (optional but good habit)

You do **not** need it for:
- General walking around the plant
- Dry electrical work or panel work
- Office/control room time

### How to activate Water Lock

1. Swipe **down** from the watch face to open the Quick Settings panel
2. Tap the **Water Lock** icon (looks like a water droplet)
3. The screen locks — touch input is disabled
4. A water drop animation confirms it's active

### How to exit Water Lock after wet conditions

1. Press and hold the **Home button** for 2–3 seconds
2. The watch vibrates rapidly to expel water from the speaker
3. Touchscreen is restored

### Optional: Add Water Lock to Quick Button (double press)

If you're activating it frequently:
1. Galaxy Wearable → Watch settings → Buttons and gestures → Quick button → Double press
2. Set to **Water Lock**
3. Now double-press activates it instantly — no swipe menu needed

### Important notes for plant-floor wear

- The watch is safe for **100m / 10 ATM** — more than adequate for washdown environments
- **Rinse with fresh water** after exposure to cleaning chemicals or sanitizers (chlorine, alcohol-based sanitizers degrade the band over time)
- The watch is **not rated for autoclaving or steam sterilization** — remove before any high-pressure steam processes
- If safety protocols at Argenta require removing the watch for confined space entry: keep it in a pocket (not loose on a surface you'll forget) — use **Samsung Find** via your phone if misplaced

## 9. Daily Vitals Logging Routine (BP + Glucose + Steps)

**Why:** Your daily note template has `bp_systolic:`, `bp_diastolic:`, `pulse:`, `glucose:`, `glucose_context:`, `weight:`, `steps:`, `sleep_hours:`, and `energy:` fields. This is ambitious tracking — here's a time-efficient routine that keeps it sustainable.

### Morning routine (5 minutes, before leaving bed area)

This sequence takes advantage of the physiological context of just waking up — resting HR and BP are most consistent first thing, before caffeine or movement.

**1. Blood pressure (2 min):**
- Still seated on bed edge or at desk
- Open **Samsung Health Monitor** on watch → Blood Pressure → Measure
- Log result to daily note: `bp_systolic:`, `bp_diastolic:`, `pulse:`

**2. Weight (30 sec, if tracking):**
- Step on scale immediately (consistent pre-eating, pre-hydration timing)
- Log: `weight:`

**3. Glucose (if monitoring — 30 sec):**
- If using a CGM (continuous glucose monitor): check the current reading from your CGM app before eating
- If using fingerstick: test before breakfast
- Log: `glucose:` (the number) and `glucose_context:` (e.g., "fasting", "2h post-dinner", "pre-workout")
- Note: the watch does not measure glucose directly — this comes from your CGM app or fingerstick device

**4. Sleep hours (automatic):**
- Samsung Health auto-calculated overnight
- Check the Sleep card in Samsung Health → note `sleep_hours:`
- Also note `energy:` — how you feel on a 1–5 scale (your quest Month 2 milestone is to track this daily)

### End-of-day routine (2 minutes)

**5. Steps:**
- Samsung Health → Activity → today's step count
- Log: `steps:`

**6. End-of-day reflection:**
- Fill `end_of_day:` in your daily note with one sentence
- Check your 3 quest habit checkboxes — moved 30+ min, hit bedtime, diet

### Weekly logging habit

Once per week (Sunday, during weekly review):
- Run **Body Composition** scan (Home + Back buttons on watch, 15 seconds)
- Log results in a note or add `body_fat_pct:` field to your daily note template if you want trend data
- Glance at Samsung Health weekly summaries → pull any notable numbers into your W-review notes

### Vault quick reference for daily fields

| Field | When to fill | Source |
|---|---|---|
| `bp_systolic:` | Morning, before eating | Samsung Health Monitor on watch |
| `bp_diastolic:` | Morning, before eating | Samsung Health Monitor on watch |
| `pulse:` | Morning (auto from BP reading) | Samsung Health Monitor on watch |
| `glucose:` | Morning fasting or per CGM | CGM app or fingerstick |
| `glucose_context:` | Same time as glucose | Manual — note timing context |
| `weight:` | Morning, before eating | Scale |
| `sleep_hours:` | Morning | Samsung Health → Sleep card |
| `energy:` | Morning (1–5 scale) | Self-rated after reviewing sleep |
| `steps:` | End of day | Samsung Health → Activity |
