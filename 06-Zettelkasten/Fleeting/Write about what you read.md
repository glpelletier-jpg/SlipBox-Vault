# Write about what you read

It’s important to write about what you read:

- because [Understanding requires effortful engagement](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zX1WtJ4ouE8sjN1NgWHsGVg8ZnVfp5Kz74Vs)

    - and in particular, [Writing forces sharper understanding](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z8q1K5a8i95qARkpFwS45qqtQzM8th82TkeUg)

    - remember: [Collecting material feels more useful than it usually is](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z8QSUyNdq3CMK79KSnCW7QTR1MPHEFi4Q2LY8)

- because otherwise you won’t [Do your own thinking](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z4enRPbLXdD8X8hCfVjaRkcGkronvhcfrgSQw)

- because [Knowledge work should accrete](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z6UDDkom8Aifg6mLdjT1sPtbMBweCmpyTwmJT) and [Evergreen note-writing helps reading efforts accumulate](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z6M8kex6kDF2FT6MWqAMDQddsqUr8sphLmyy1)

- indeed: [Most people read ineffectively](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z432siNjuY9G8bTsnSugyHPB1YoZWgup6eMB3)

It’s not enough to just highlight or write marginalia in books: there isn’t much pressure to synthesize, connect, or to get to the heart of things. And they don’t add up to anything over time as you read more. Instead, write [Evergreen notes](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z4SDCZQeRo4xFEQ8H4qrSqd68ucpgE6LU155C) as you read.

On the other hand, it’s worth noting: [The most effective readers and thinkers I know don’t take notes when reading](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z6GNVv6RyFDewy11ZgXzce8agWxSLwJ6Ub5Rw). Speaking at least for myself, experience has suggested that I need more support to effectively engage with what I’m reading.

## Method

Our broad approach is an alternating cycle:

1. **Collect** passages that seem interesting and thoughts that emerge while reading: [How to collect observations while reading](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z71FeBJGqZdyA78UNTwXCWcMGUVCWk1XsLvPS)

2. **Process** clusters of those passages and thoughts into lasting notes:[How to process reading annotations into evergreen notes](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z2PJ51tCXuPFxnfFVUxxgwjvZ1geu4YnYm7hK)
