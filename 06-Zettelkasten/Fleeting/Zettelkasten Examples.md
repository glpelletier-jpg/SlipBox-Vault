## Zettelkasten Examples

Examples of note types

- **Fleeting notes:** Quick, temporary notes to capture an idea as it comes to you.

    - **Example:** A sentence jotted down on a napkin or in a digital note-taking app about a new writing technique you want to try.

- **Literature notes:** Concise summaries of ideas from a source, written in your own words.

    - **Example:** A literature note on the "butterfly effect" could summarize the concept from a book and include the source details (book title, page number).

    - **Example:** A note on a podcast that includes the time stamp and speaker's name.

- **Permanent notes:** Your own thoughts and insights, linked to literature notes or other permanent notes. This is the core of the Zettelkasten.

    - **Example:** A permanent note that connects the "butterfly effect" concept to the "Kindness Pandemic" Facebook group, explaining how both show the power of small actions creating large effects.

- **Project notes:** Notes created for a specific project that don't necessarily fit into the main Zettelkasten.

    - **Example:** Author Ryan Holiday creates a separate Zettelkasten for each book he writes, with notes organized by folders.

- [Index notes](https://www.google.com/search?sca_esv=0cb097f5cf89beeb&cs=1&q=Index+notes&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwjkhNG2xNuQAxWZL9AFHbPLHFQQgK4QegQIAxAK&biw=2560&bih=1271&dpr=1.5&mstk=AUtExfA8dGpGNSwgT6fiLw-kUr3uziI6Gn0asMErii3hvImq2NGHJxLYQHuXTeIiHnIQ5QxMKodDcsZBoBGJSBhtq8E0rvWPqp6bDXPleQqZktoRu0zPkqn964Y0vL0tUpJLicHhAakdJ13qBQovMQIQ9xmU_SajHiWW339oarUW_sz2zG8&csui=3)**:** Notes that act as a table of contents for a specific topic, linking to all related permanent notes.

    - **Example:** An index note for "Confucianism" that links to various other notes about specific Confucian concepts, like "Confucian analytics".
