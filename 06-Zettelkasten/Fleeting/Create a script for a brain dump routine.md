# Create a script for a brain dump routine

Here's your guided brain dump — 7 prompts across 4 phases:

**Unload** → get everything out of your head, unfiltered.
**Feel it** → check in on what's actually draining you emotionally.
**Sort it out** → separate what matters from what can wait.
**Land** → close with a single intention rather than a new to-do pile.

Work through it at whatever pace feels right — there's no timer. When you finish, hit "Spot patterns ↗" and I can help you pull out any recurring themes, things to act on, or anything worth sitting with longer.
