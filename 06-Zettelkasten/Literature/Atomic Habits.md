## Key ideas (in your own words)

1. Habits are the compound interest of self-improvement. A 1% daily improvement doesn't feel like anything in the moment — but over a year it produces results that feel like overnight success. This is exactly why the Health Reset starts with just showing up (30 min movement) rather than trying to transform everything at once.

2. The four-step habit loop — cue, craving, response, reward — is the mechanism behind every behavior. To build a new habit: make it obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying. To break a bad one, invert each step. Environment design beats willpower every time.

3. Identity beats outcomes. 'I want to lose weight' fails when motivation dips. 'I am someone who exercises daily' survives because every workout is evidence for who you are. The Health Reset is really about becoming someone whose health is a foundation — not completing a 90-day program.
