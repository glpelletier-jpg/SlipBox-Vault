## Core tensions

- **Motivation vs. systems** — motivation fluctuates; systems persist. Atomic Habits' central argument.
- **Insight vs. behavior change** — understanding *why* you do something doesn't automatically change it (Dr K's recurring theme)
- **Flow as goal vs. flow as byproduct** — you can't force flow, only create conditions for it
- **Identity vs. outcome** — changing who you are vs. what you do; which drives lasting change?
